How it Started.....

The cacao tree was cultivated more than 3,000 years ago by the Maya, Toltec, and Aztec peoples, who prepared a beverage from its fruit ie. the cocoa bean hence sometimes using it as a ceremonial drink and also used the bean as a currency.The Maya considered chocolate to be the food of the gods, held the cacao tree to be sacred.
